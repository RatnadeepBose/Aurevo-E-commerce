How to Measure

Chest

Measure around the fullest part of your chest, keeping the tape horizontal

Length

Measure from the highest point of the shoulder to the bottom hem

Shoulder

Measure from shoulder seam to shoulder seam across the back

Sleeve

Measure from shoulder seam to the end of the sleeve

Important Notes

• All measurements are in inches

• Measurements may vary slightly (±1 inch) due to fabric characteristics

• For the best fit, compare your body measurements with our size chart

• If you're between sizes, we recommend choosing the larger size
